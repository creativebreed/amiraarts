# AmiraArts - Art Workshop & Gallery Platform

London's premiere boutique studio for fine art education and original curation.

## Features
- ✅ Workshop booking system
- ✅ Art gallery & e-commerce
- ✅ Multi-role dashboards (Admin, Artist, Customer)
- ✅ Reviews & ratings
- ✅ Wishlist & favorites
- ✅ Social sharing
- ✅ Gift cards & promo codes
- ✅ PWA support
- ✅ Multi-language (EN, FR, ES, DE, IT)
- ✅ SEO optimized
- ✅ WCAG AA accessible
